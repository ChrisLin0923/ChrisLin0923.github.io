Warmth

Though the same sun shines upon us, sometimes only children really pay attention to the world around us. The contrast between the light and shadow of this photo reveals a compelling story. The curiosity of children seems to be blessed by the warmth of the sun.And I think as a photographer, we strive to have the eyes of children. Sometimes appreciating and viewing this world through a child's eyes makes the world profoundly colorful.
