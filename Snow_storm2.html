Snow Storm

Me and my crew have been anticipating a snow storm to hit New York City, so that we can have a photoshoot on the streets of Manhattan. When that snow storm arrived, we packed our gear and headed towards the city. From afternoon into late night, we took this photo in the middle of the Manhattan street, the fog coming from underground combined with the snow gave this photo a cinematic scenery.
